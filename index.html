Meet the Team

Housed under Bayanihan, formerly known as the Filipino Student Association, Haluan is the only competing Hip Hop Dance team at UCSC. Haluan performs at numerous events on campus such as the Holi Festival and participates in competitions such as Collaboration NorCal. Haluan also hosts the En Route Urban Dace Showcase, the only exhibition show if its kind in the area. We strive to foster an environment of inclusivity and creativity through our shared love for Dance.
